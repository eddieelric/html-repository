The Best Movies According to Me :D

My top 3 movies of all time

Blade Runner

Blade Runner is my favorite movie. I found out about it when I was really young. It tooks years for me to finally watch it, but ever since, it has become one of my favorite pieces of media

Parasite

While fairly new, Parasite is a perfect example of capitalisms decadence in general. Bein from South Korea, they are able to really transmit the feeling on what it's like to live in an ultra capitalist hellscape

Roma

This one is not so much about the movie itself, rather the feelings it evokes in me. The movie is kind of like a collection of smaller movies put together that show the most important moments of the director's life. It makes me wonder: What would my Roma look like? What would I include? How would I name it? It really makes me think...
